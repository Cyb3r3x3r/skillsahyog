üîπ Step-by-Step Guide to Your First Skill Exchange

1. Create Your Account

Sign up on SkillSahyog with your email address and basic details. Your account will allow you to showcase your skills and connect with others in the community.

2. Complete Your Profile

Add your profile picture, a short bio, location, and list the skills you can offer or want to learn. The more detailed your profile, the easier it is to match with the right users.

3. Browse or Search for Skills

Explore available skills by going to the Skill Matching page. You can search user by choosing the skill from the dropdown you want help with and a list of users sorted based on AI algorithm will be displayed to you.

4. Send an Exchange Request

Once you find a match, send an exchange request by going to their profile. Also keep in mind it is recommended to send request to only verified users. For example, you can offer Python help in exchange for Cloud computing.

5. Chat & Collaborate

When your request is accepted, a private chatroom will be created for both of you. You can access you chatroom from your dashboard. Use this space to discuss schedules and learn from each other.

6. Complete the Exchange & Leave Feedback

Once your exchange is complete, leave a rating and feedback for your partner. An automatic AI generated score will be assigned to both of you based on the ratings you have received.The feedback system helps maintain trust and improves user rankings.
