Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

SkillSahyog values your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Username, email, date of birth, profile details.
  • Skill Data: Skills offered/received, ratings, feedback.
  • Chat Data: Messages exchanged in skill chats.
  • Device & Usage Data: IP address, browser info (standard logs).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To match you with skill partners.
  • To personalize user experience.
  • To improve platform functionality.
  • To send real-time notifications.
  • To maintain safety and integrity.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Limited information is shared with:

  • Notification services (e.g., Google Cloud Pub/Sub).
  • Authorized service providers for platform support.

5. Data Retention

Your data is stored securely and retained as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services.

6. Security

We use industry-standard measures (encryption, access control) to protect your data.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to your data, correction of inaccuracies, or deletion of your profile at any time.

For any concerns, contact us at our email .
